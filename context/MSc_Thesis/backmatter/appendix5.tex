\chapter{Additional Self-Refine Results} \label{ap5:self_refine}

This appendix presents supplementary material for the Self-Refine results of Section~\ref{sec:results-self-refine}. Section~\ref{ap5:effect} breaks the refinement effect down by individual game $\times$ seat $\times$ tier cell and by whether the refined seat is structurally disadvantaged; Section~\ref{ap5:completion} reports the completion rate by condition; and Section~\ref{ap5:mechanism} examines the direction of the revisions themselves.

\section{Effect by Cell and Seat Type} \label{ap5:effect}

Figure~\ref{fig:refine-default-scatter} compares each refined seat against its \texttt{DD} baseline cell by cell, and Figure~\ref{fig:refine-structural-disadvantaged} regroups the same effect by whether the refined seat is structurally disadvantaged.

\begin{figure}[ht]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=0.9\linewidth]{figures/results/self_refine/default_vs_refine_scatter_cells.png}
    \caption{Default-versus-refined comparison for each game $\times$ seat $\times$ tier
    cell. Points above the diagonal indicate that refining the seat improved that seat
    relative to its \texttt{DD} baseline. Highlighted cells mark bootstrap 95\% confidence
    intervals on the delta that exclude zero.}
    \label{fig:refine-default-scatter}
\end{figure}

\begin{figure}[ht]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=0.8\linewidth]{figures/results/self_refine/refine_structural_disadvantaged_effects.png}
    \caption{Self-Refine effect grouped by whether the refined seat is structurally
    disadvantaged. The aggregate pattern is positive for disadvantaged seats and negative
    for already-advantaged seats, though most individual cells remain statistically
    indistinguishable from zero.}
    \label{fig:refine-structural-disadvantaged}
\end{figure}

\section{Revision Direction} \label{ap5:mechanism}

Figure~\ref{fig:refine-mechanism-disadvantaged} classifies each revision in a structurally disadvantaged seat as hardening, softening, unchanged, or no proposal, isolating the direction in which Self-Refine moves the agent's offer.

\begin{figure}[ht]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=0.8\linewidth]{figures/results/self_refine/refine_mechanism_disadvantaged_direction.png}
    \caption{Revision direction in structurally disadvantaged seats. A revision is counted
    as hardening when it raises the refined agent's own payoff-if-accepted, softening when
    it lowers it, unchanged when the proposal stays fixed, and no proposal when no parseable
    proposal is available for the comparison.}
    \label{fig:refine-mechanism-disadvantaged}
\end{figure}
